Getting there
Barcelona → Sitges by Train
🚆
Rodalies Line R2 Sud · ~40 minutes
Trains run every 20–30 min — no need to book in advance
1
Head to Passeig de Gràcia or Sants station
Both stations have frequent R2 Sud departures. Sants has more trains and is slightly faster.
2
Take the R2 Sud line towards Sant Vicenç de Calders
Look for the R2 Sud on the departure board. The train runs along the coast — grab a window seat!
~40 min from Sants · ~50 min from Passeig de Gràcia
3
Get off at Sitges station
The BnBs are a 10–15 min walk from the station, or a quick taxi ride. Follow signs to the old town (Carrer de la Davallada, 7).
Carrer de la Davallada, 7 — BnB address
4
Tap your T-Mobilitat card at the barriers
The R2 Sud is covered — just tap in at departure and tap out when you arrive in Sitges.
Return journey: last train ~11:30pm
